# 📊 Superstore Sales Performance Analysis (Microsoft Excel)

## Overview

This project is an interactive sales dashboard built entirely in **Microsoft Excel** using a subset of the Superstore dataset (Furniture category). The goal was to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business insights through data cleaning, PivotTables, PivotCharts, KPIs, and interactive slicers.

The dashboard enables users to explore sales and profitability across regions and product sub-categories while monitoring operational metrics such as shipping time and discount levels.

---

## Business Problem

A business may generate strong sales but still struggle with profitability.

This dashboard investigates whether sales performance translates into profit by answering questions such as:

- Which regions generate the highest sales?
- Which product sub-categories are making or losing money?
- How does profit change over time?
- What is the average customer discount?
- How efficiently are orders being shipped?

## Key Findings

Analysis of the dashboard revealed several business insights:

- Total Sales reached **30,924.56**.
- Despite strong sales, the business recorded an overall **loss of 811.36**, indicating profitability challenges.
- The **South** region generated the highest sales, followed by the **East** region.
- **Tables** recorded the largest losses among all furniture sub-categories.
- The average customer discount was approximately **14.7%**.
- Orders were delivered in an average of **4.3 days**.
- Monthly profit fluctuated significantly, with only a few months showing positive returns.

These findings suggest that increasing sales alone does not guarantee profitability and that discounting and product mix should be reviewed.
